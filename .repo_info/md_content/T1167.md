# T1167

**ID:** T1167

**Name:** Extract search terms from URLs

**Objective(s):** Extract artifacts, or content of specific types

**Description:**

Parsing a URL and extracting search terms from the parameters.

**Synonyms:**



**Details:**

These may be `q=` strings or some other encoded parameters. 

This would include web search URLs such as Google searches but could also include search terms entered into other sites such as travel or shopping sites.

**Subtechniques:**



**Examples:**

- Google search URLs
- DuckDuckGo search URLs


**Input Classes:**

- [https://ontology.unifiedcyberontology.org/uco/observable/URLHistory](https://ontology.unifiedcyberontology.org/uco/observable/URLHistory)

**Output Classes:**
